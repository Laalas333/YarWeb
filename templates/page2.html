About us

YarWeb is an innovative web-based application designed to enhance your digital security by analyzing executable files (.exe) for potential threats.

YarWeb stands as your digital sentinel, meticulously scanning executable files to detect any traces of malicious code. Our platform is built upon a robust signature-matching technology that sifts through executables with precision, ensuring that threats are identified and neutralized.

How YarWeb Works

Signature Database: At YarWeb's foundation lies a comprehensive database brimming with signature profiles of known malware. These unique identifiers are the fingerprints of malware, allowing YarWeb to detect malicious patterns within files accurately.

Generating YARA Rules: The YARA tool is integral to YarWeb, employing a rule-based approach to uncover malware. By generating customized YARA rules, YarWeb harnesses the collective intelligence of our extensive signature database to spot and flag potential dangers.

File Upload and Analysis: YarWeb empowers users to directly upload their .exe files for analysis. Upon upload, YarWeb engages its YARA-based scanning engine to dissect the file, comparing extracted strings against our vast database to determine the file's safety.

Threshold Matching and Results: YarWeb establishes a threshold for threat detection, ensuring that only files with a significant match to known malware signatures are flagged, reducing the likelihood of false positives.

Reporting Malicious Strings: Detection is only the beginning. YarWeb goes a step further by providing detailed reports on any malicious strings found, equipping users with the knowledge needed for targeted response and prevention.
